Intrusion Detection Using URL Analysis

Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) leverage URL analysis to detect and prevent unauthorized access and malicious activities online. By scrutinizing URLs, IDS can identify potential threats based on various indicators. These indicators include:

  • Suspicious URL patterns that deviate from legitimate domains.
  • URLs containing known malicious keywords or anomalous structures.
  • Redirection chains that lead to untrusted or blacklisted sites.
  • Use of shortened URLs that obscure the true destination.
  • URLs associated with known attack vectors or previously flagged IP addresses.

By analyzing these elements, IDS can effectively distinguish between benign and malicious URLs, thereby protecting users from potential security breaches and ensuring the integrity of the network.
